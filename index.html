FAQs

Find answers to common questions about CDS's online document storage services and policies.

How does it work?

CDS's online document storage works by securely storing your files in the cloud. You can access your documents from anywhere, at any time, using any device with an internet connection. Our advanced encryption ensures that your files are protected and can only be accessed by you.

What are the pricing options?

CDS offers different online document storage packages to suit your needs. We have 5 Gig storage for R99 00 a month, 10 Gig for R180 00 a month, and 15 Gig for R260 00 a month. If you require more storage, please contact our sales team for a customized solution.

Is my data secure?

Yes, your data is secure with CDS. We use industry-standard encryption protocols to protect your files from unauthorized access. Our servers are located in secure data centers with multiple layers of physical and digital security measures in place.

How can I upgrade my storage?

To upgrade your storage, please contact our help desk. Our friendly support team will assist you in upgrading your storage package to meet your growing needs.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Simply contact our customer support team, and they will guide you through the cancellation process. Please note that cancellation may result in the loss of your stored documents.

How do i lock content in a document?

Highlight the content you wish to lock as if you want to copy it, a dialog will pop out, scroll down and you will see "Lock content". Locked content appear blurry.

Contact our our support team for assistance.
